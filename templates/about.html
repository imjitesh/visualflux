What is Cartographr

Cartographr's aim is to provide the world with a platform where
all the data is provided in the form of charts and visualizations.

Consider Cartographr as a Wikipedia of Visualisations, anyone can embed
the charts available here, prepare charts using predefined visualizations options,
and edit already available visualizations.

Use it well.
